How to manage induction, training and support for early career teachers (ECTs)

{% include "guidance/_table-of-contents.html" %}

{{ pageTitle }}

Use the Manage training for early career teachers service to:

  • check if early career teachers (ECTs) are eligible to serve statutory induction, and receive DfE funding for training and support based on the early career framework (ECF)
  • check if mentors working with ECTs are eligible for DfE funding for ECF-based training and time off timetable
  • get access to ECF-based training materials for ECTs and their mentors

Schools in England must use this service to:

  • nominate one person as their registered user (or 'induction tutor')
  • report how they'll provide ECF-based training for ECTs each academic year
  • register each ECT and their mentor
  • report which mentor each ECT will work with to ensure the correct funding is paid
  • report if a registered ECT or mentor transfers to another school in England during their ECF-based induction or training

Your schools will need to provide some of this information to your appropriate body and training provider too, if you work with one.
