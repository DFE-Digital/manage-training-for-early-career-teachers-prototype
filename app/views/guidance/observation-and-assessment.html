How to manage induction, training and support for early career teachers (ECTs)

Throughout statutory induction you must:

  • arrange for each ECT’s teaching to be observed at regular intervals make sure ECTs and mentors have adequate time off timetable to participate fully in ECF-based training and mentoring
  • following any other instructions from your [appropriate body](/appoint-appropriate-body) or [training provider](/training-option#use-a-lead-provider)

In terms 1, 2, 4 and 5 of a standard 2-year induction, you must arrange progress reviews against the [Teachers’ Standards](https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/teachers-standards) for each ECT.

In terms 3 and 6 of a standard 2-year induction, you must arrange for each ECT to be formally assessed by your school’s [induction tutor](/induction-tutor) or headteacher.

See section 2 of the statutory guidance for information on how to complete these tasks.

Adapting training and induction for ECTs working part time, or completing a reduced induction

If any ECTs are serving a reduced or part-time induction at your school, your headteacher and appropriate body should decide on the best ECF-based training, progress review and assessment schedule for them. This must also be shared with your training provider if you work with one.

See guidance on [proportionate funding](/funding-eligibility#proportionate-funding) for these ECTs.

For ECTs completing their induction and ECF-based training, follow:

  • your appropriate body’s instructions to submit a final assessment for each ECT
  • any other instructions from your appropriate body or training provider

You do not need to withdraw or delete their information from the [DfE service](/report-to-dfe). Your training provider and appropriate body will confirm that they’ve completed training and induction, and we’ll update their records for you.

For ECTs starting their induction and ECF-based training in the next academic year:

  • appoint an appropriate body for each ECT
  • if you want to work with a training provider, [contact them](/training-options#use-a-lead-provider) to confirm their availability

Then sign in to the Manage training for early career teachers service to:

  • tell us how your school will deliver ECF-based training in the next academic year
  • register ECTs starting their statutory induction and ECF-based training at your school
  • register mentors starting their ECF-based training at your school
  • assign each ECT a mentor
