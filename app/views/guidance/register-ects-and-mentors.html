Early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors taking part in ECF-based training must be registered with the Department for Education (DfE) if your school:

  • uses a training provider funded by the DfE
  • delivers your own training using DfE-accredited materials

We need this information to confirm their eligibility for funding, and arrange access to training and materials.

Your school does not need to register ECTs and mentors with the DfE if you choose to:

  • design and deliver your own training
  • work with a training provider funded directly by your school (if your school is not eligible for DfE-funded training, for example)

How to register an ECT or mentor with the DfE

Your school’s registered induction tutor must sign into the Manage training for early career teachers service to register each ECT and mentor.

You’ll be asked to enter their:

  • full name
  • teacher reference number
  • date of birth
  • email address (personal or school)
  • start date at your school, if they’re moving from another school

If your school does not have this information, contact the person directly to ask for it.

Assign each ECT a mentor

Your school’s registered induction tutor must also use the Manage training for early career teachers service to tell us which mentor will work with each ECT.
