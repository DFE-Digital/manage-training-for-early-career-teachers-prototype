How to manage induction, training and support for early career teachers (ECTs)

{% include "guidance/_table-of-contents.html" %}

{{ pageTitle }}

Your school’s registered induction tutor should sign in to the Manage training for early career teachers service to report if:

  • a new ECT or mentor begins their induction or ECF-based training part-way through the year
  • an ECT or mentor transfers to your school, or moves to another school, part-way through their induction or ECF-based training
  • you no longer expect any ECTs to start their statutory induction at your school that year
  • the headteacher wants someone else to be registered as your school’s induction tutor instead (it’s important to do this before you leave your role or the school)
  • an ECT or mentor’s name or email address changes

If your school works with a training provider, contact them directly to:

  • tell them you want to work with a different training provider instead (they’ll report this to us)
  • withdraw or defer an ECT or mentor who has left teaching, is taking a break, or takes a teaching job outside of England (for ECTs, you must report this to their appropriate body too)
  • reinstate an ECT or mentor who was previously withdrawn from training

Contact the DfE support team if you:

  • want to change your ECF-based training option
  • want to change your DfE-accredited training materials
