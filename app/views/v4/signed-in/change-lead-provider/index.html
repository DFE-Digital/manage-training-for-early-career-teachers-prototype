If you want to change your lead provider (opens in a new window or tab) you need to:

  • contact your current provider to let them know you want to end the partnership
  • sign up with your new lead provider

Your new provider will then report the change to us.

If you've already contacted your providers

If you contacted your current and new lead provider more than 2 weeks ago, and the change has still not been made, contact us.
