Manage training for early career teachers

Set up and manage early career framework (ECF)-based training at your school.

Use this service to report:

  • details about your early career teachers (ECTs) and mentors
  • which appropriate body you've appointed
  • who you've nominated as your induction tutor
  • which training option you've chosen

Your school must complete these steps before your ECTs can start their statutory induction programme.

You can also use this service to report any changes, for example, nominating a new induction tutor for your school.

Sign in if you've been nominated as the induction tutor for your school.
